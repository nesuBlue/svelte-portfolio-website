# svelte-portfolio-website

A personal portfolio site I have been meaning to put together for a while now. It is live at [nesublue.github.io/svelte-portfolio-website](https://nesublue.github.io/svelte-portfolio-website/).

---

## About Me

I am a postgraduate computer scientist with a focus on virtual reality and audio spatialisation. Alongside my research and technical work I am also a musician and a tinkerer, which means I tend to end up at the intersection of sound, space, and code more often than not.

My interests span immersive audio environments, 3D sound rendering, and the ways spatial audio can enhance presence inside virtual worlds. When I am not deep in a research problem I am usually making music, pulling apart some piece of hardware, or building something just to see if it works.

---

## The Site

This portfolio covers my background, projects, and skills. It is intended as a living document that I will keep updated as my work evolves.

**Live site:** [nesublue.github.io/svelte-portfolio-website](https://nesublue.github.io/svelte-portfolio-website/)

---

## Built With

This site is built on top of the excellent [slick-portfolio-svelte-5](https://github.com/RiadhAdrani/slick-portfolio-svelte-5) template by [RiadhAdrani](https://github.com/RiadhAdrani). It uses:

- [SvelteKit](https://kit.svelte.dev/) with Svelte 5
- [shadcn-svelte](https://www.shadcn-svelte.com/) for UI components
- [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) for styling
- [UnoCSS](https://unocss.dev/) for fonts and icons
- TypeScript throughout

---
